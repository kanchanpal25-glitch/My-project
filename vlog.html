Learning To Bake: My First Loaf

By Asha Kumar — October 27, 2025

Last weekend I decided to try baking bread at home for the very first time. I followed a simple recipe and learned a few things along the way — some mistakes and some happy surprises!

Ingredients I Used

Flour, water, yeast, salt, and a little patience. That’s all you need to begin.

Step-by-step (short)

Mix the ingredients, knead the dough, let it rise, shape it, and then bake. Rest, then enjoy.

"Baking is both a science and an art — the oven is your lab and your canvas." — Unknown

What I Learned

The dough needs a warm place to rise. Be gentle while shaping. And always, always preheat the oven.
