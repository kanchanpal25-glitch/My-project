Classic Masala Pasta

A quick, spicy pasta that's great for weeknights - ready in 20 minutes.

Ingredients

Serves

  • 200g pasta (penne or fusilli)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tsp chill popped
  • saltto taste
  • fresh corianderfor granish

Instructions

    -
  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente, then drain.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Saute onion until translucent.
  3. Add tomato and spices; cook until soft.
  4. toss in the cooked pasta and mix well to coat with the masala.
  5. serve hot, grenish with coriander.server immediateil

notes & tips

If you like it creamier, add a splash of milk or a small dollp of butter before serving
